About us

Who are we?

Founded in 2019, GS Construct AB is a construction company that performs all common work in construction, renovation, and service work on new constructions, conversions and extensions. We mainly operate in Kronoberg, but our coverage extends to Kalmar, Jönköping and other neighboring counties. We professionally perform various types of construction work such as flooring parquet or concrete floors, unfolding walls with both wooden and steel frames, plastering, various carpentry or concrete works and the installation of interior doors and windows.

Why should you choose us?

Despite being a start-up, GS Construct AB has a professionally certificated crew with education and experience in the construction industry, from our architects to our construction workers.
